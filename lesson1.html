What is Airbnb?

Airbnb is a global online platform that connects travelers with hosts offering unique accommodations worldwide. Founded in 2008, Airbnb has revolutionized travel by allowing people to rent out their homes or rooms and experience stays beyond traditional hotels.

How Does Airbnb Work?

  • Guests can search, review, and book listings tailored to their needs and budget.
  • Hosts list their properties, set availability and pricing, and welcome guests from around the globe.

Benefits of Airbnb

  • Access to a vast variety of unique and affordable places to stay
  • Trusted reviews and secure payments
  • Flexible hosting opportunities to earn extra income
  • Direct communication through the platform for safety and convenience

Extended Description

This lesson provides a comprehensive introduction to Airbnb, highlighting its origins, core functionality, and benefits for both guests and hosts. It sets the stage for understanding how the platform operates, its impact on the travel industry, and what you can expect to learn in this course.

Whether you're interested in becoming a guest or a host, this foundational knowledge will help you navigate Airbnb with confidence and make the most of the upcoming lessons.
